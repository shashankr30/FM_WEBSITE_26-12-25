Subsystems

Breakdown of our departments.

SYS 01

Structures

The backbone of the car, ensuring rigidity and safety under extreme loads.

SYS 02

Aerodynamics

Manipulating airflow to generate downforce and minimize drag.

SYS 03

Vehicle Dynamics

Optimizing suspension geometry and tire utilization for peak handling.

SYS 04

Transmission

Efficiently transferring power from the engine to the wheels.

SYS 05

E-Powertrain

Designing, optimizing, and managing the complete powertrain architecture.

SYS 06

Electronics

Managing sensor acquisition, safety interlocks, HV/LV monitoring, and CAN.

SYS 07

Driverless

Pioneering autonomous racing with advanced perception and path planning.

SYS 08

Management

Ensuring logistics, finances, and marketing drive the team forward.
